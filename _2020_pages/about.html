Purpose

The purpose: generalization of experience for development and implementation of regional and spatial planning policy, urban management, design of integrated development plans, legal regulation in urban planning and urban design, the formation of an innovative model for training lawyers and political analysts in urban planning and construction.

FIRST INTERNATIONAL RESEARCH-TO-PRACTICE CONFERENCE "SPATIAL PLANNING: URBAN PLANNING, ARCHITECTURE, POLITICAL AND SOCIO-CULTURAL PRINCIPLES"

Subjects:

  • Urban planning and spatial planning: theoretical principles, future prospects for the development
  • Architecture in the context of spatial planning
  • Political, historical and cultural development principles of cities and regions
  • The design and implementation of integrated development projects
  • The impact of the pandemic on development of cities and society

FOURTH INTERNATIONAL RESEARCH-TO-PRACTICE CONFERENCE "THE BUILDING LAW: THEORY AND PRACTICE OF URBAN PLANNING AND URBAN DESIGN"

Тематика:

  • The state of law support for urban planning and urban design
  • Institutes of civil, administrative, land use, financial, environmental and criminal law in the field of construction, urban planning and architecture
  • Current issues of construction law modernization
  • Tasks for law education in Universities those train architecture and civil engineering students
